about

Hey there! I'm Alex, a full-stack developer and product designer who gets excited about building things that people actually want to use. Currently based in Austin, Texas, where I spend my days turning complex problems into simple, elegant digital solutions.

My journey started somewhat accidentally—I was studying economics in college but kept getting distracted by how terribly designed most university websites were. One thing led to another, and I found myself teaching myself HTML and CSS at 2 AM instead of studying for my macro exam. Best life decision ever.

Over the past eight years, I've had the privilege of working with everyone from anxious startup founders to Fortune 500 executives, helping them translate their wildest ideas into digital products that actually work. I've learned that the best technology is invisible—it just helps people get things done without getting in the way.

What I Do

Frontend Magic: React, TypeScript, Next.js, Vue.js, and whatever new framework the internet is excited about this week

Backend Systems: Node.js, Python, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, AWS, and the occasional venture into DevOps territory

Design & UX: Figma, user research, prototyping, and the gentle art of making complicated things feel simple

Product Strategy: Turning "wouldn't it be cool if..." into "here's exactly how we'll build and measure this"

When I'm Not Coding

You'll find me exploring Austin's food truck scene (current obsession: Korean-Mexican fusion), attempting to keep my houseplants alive (success rate: improving), or disappearing into national parks with my camera and hiking boots.

I'm also slightly obsessed with mechanical keyboards (currently typing on a custom 60% with Cherry MX Blues), pour-over coffee methods, and collecting vinyl records of bands that probably have fewer monthly Spotify listeners than my last app had users.

I believe the best products come from diverse teams, honest feedback, and the willingness to throw away your first three ideas because the fourth one is always better. Let's build something amazing together.
